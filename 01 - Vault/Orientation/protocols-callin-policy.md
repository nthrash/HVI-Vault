**Call-in Violation:** 
- A Verbal Warning for performance/behavior is recommended upon the first Call-in Violation. If a previous disciplinary action for performance/behavior has been issued, then progressive disciplinary action is recommended.

**NCNS (No Call No show)** **Violation**: A Final Written Warning for performance/behavior is recommended upon the first NCNS. If a previous Final Written Warning for performance/behavior has been issued, then separation of employment is recommended. If an employee is within their first 90 days of employment, separation of employment is recommended upon the first NCNS.

**If an employee does not call-in before the start of their shift and arrives late to their shift,** **the employee is subject to discipline under the Disciplinary Action for Attendance and** **Punctuality policy.**
