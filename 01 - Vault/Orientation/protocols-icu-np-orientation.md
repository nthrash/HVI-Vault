* **Study:** RV Pressure-Volume loops. Protek Duo migration risks. HIT management.

### Weeks 11-12: Transplant Immunology
* **Clinical:** Induction therapy (Basiliximab/Thymo). Acute Rejection surveillance. Denervated heart physiology.
* **Procedures:** Consolidation (Aim for 10+ lines). Vent weaning parameters.
* **Study:** ISHLT Grading for Rejection. Tacrolimus/Cyclosporine pharmacology.
